Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 9, 2026

TL;DR: We collect minimal data, don't sell your information, and prioritize your privacy. Cryptographic keys are stored locally on your device.

1. Information We Collect

Browser Extension

  • Cryptographic Keys: Generated and stored locally in your browser. Never sent to our servers.
  • Email (optional): Only if you choose to associate an identity with your key.
  • Signatures: When you create a shortlink, the signed content is stored on our servers.

Vouch Gatekeeper (GitHub App)

  • Repository Access: Read access to repository contents and pull requests.
  • Commit Data: We read commit signatures to verify identity.
  • No Storage: We do not permanently store repository content or commit data.

2. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Verify cryptographic signatures on commits
  • Store and retrieve signed content for shortlinks
  • Improve our Services

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may share data only:

  • When required by law
  • To protect our rights or property
  • With your explicit consent

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • TLS encryption for all data in transit
  • Secure cloud infrastructure (Cloudflare Workers)
  • No storage of private keys on our servers

5. Data Retention

  • Shortlink signatures: Stored for 1 year (free tier) or indefinitely (pro tier)
  • GitHub webhook data: Processed in real-time, not stored
  • Local keys: Stored until you delete them from your browser

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Export your data
  • Opt out of optional data collection

7. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Cloudflare: Hosting and CDN
  • GitHub: Repository hosting and OAuth

8. Cookies

We use minimal cookies for essential functionality only. No tracking or advertising cookies are used.

9. Children's Privacy

Our Services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

11. Contact

For privacy-related questions, please open an issue at our GitHub repository.
